      About this Addon

      This addon connects your IPTV service to Stremio. Choose from free public channels via IPTV-org, your own Xtream Codes API subscription, or any M3U/M3U+ playlist URL.

      • IPTV-org (Free) – thousands of free public channels, no credentials needed.
      • Xtream API – connects your subscription panel (no movies or series).
      • M3U / M3U+ – paste any playlist URL; EPG auto-detected from the header.
      • Optional EPG: panel XMLTV, custom XMLTV URL, or auto-detected from playlist.
      • EPG offset adjusts programme times for timezone correction.
