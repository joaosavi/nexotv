      About

      Connect your IPTV service to Stremio. Choose from free public channels, your Xtream Codes subscription, or any M3U playlist URL.

      • IPTV-org – thousands of free public channels, no credentials needed.
      • Xtream API – connects your subscription panel (live TV only).
      • M3U / M3U+ – paste any playlist URL; EPG auto-detected from header.
      • EPG – panel XMLTV, custom XMLTV URL, or auto-detected.
      • EPG offset – adjusts programme times for timezone correction.
