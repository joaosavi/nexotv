About this Addon

This addon connects your IPTV service (via Xtream Codes API) to Stremio. It fetches live TV channels and optionally loads EPG data.

  • Live TV only – fetches channels from your panel (no movies or series).
  • Optional EPG: use panel XMLTV or a custom XMLTV URL.
  • EPG offset adjusts programme times for timezone correction.
